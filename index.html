About

I’m a PhD student in Bioengineering at UC Berkeley (2019–). I completed B.S. in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics and M.S. in Computer Science both at UCLA. I worked on automated prostate cancer diagnosis from multi-parametric MRI in Magnetic Resonance Research Labs, advised by Prof. Kyung Hyun Sung and co-advised by Prof. Fabien Scalzo. During my undergrad, I worked on knowledge graph parsing/explainable AI advised by Prof. Song-Chun Zhu.

Quick note: My name is pronounced as “Rei-ming Tsao” instead of “Ru-ming Kao”.
