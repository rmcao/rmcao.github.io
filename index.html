Ruiming Cao

Graduate student

UCLA


I’m a second-year graduate student in computer science at University of California, Los Angeles. I’m working on computer-aided diagnosis for prostate cancer in Magnetic Resonance Research Labs, Radiology, supervised by Prof. Kyung Hyun Sung. I graduated with B.S. in computer science and in applied mathematics at UCLA in 2017. In college, I worked on knowledge graph parsing/explainable AI supervised by Prof. Song-Chun Zhu.

My current research interests are medical imaging, computer vision, and MRI.
