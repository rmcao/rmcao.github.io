I’m a second-year graduate student in computer science at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). I’m working on computer-aided diagnosis in Magnetic Resonance Research Labs, Radiology, supervised by Prof. Kyung Hyun Sung. I graduated with B.S. in computer science and in applied mathematics at UCLA in 2017. During my undergrad, I worked on knowledge graph parsing/explainable AI supervised by Prof. Song-Chun Zhu.
